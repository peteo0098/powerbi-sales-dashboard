# Superstore Sales Performance Dashboard

[![View on GitHub](https://img.shields.io/badge/View_on_GitHub-100000?style=for-the-badge&logo=github&logoColor=white)](https://github.com/peteo0098/powerbi-sales-dashboard)

Project Overview
This project shows how business intelligence dashboards can actively oversee company sales performance, spot growth opportunities, and monitor profitability. Utilizing the traditional Superstore Sales dataset, this interactive Power BI report offers high-level executive summaries along with detailed regional and categorical analytics.

![Dashboard Preview](https://github.com/peteo0098/powerbi-sales-dashboard/blob/main/sales-performance-dashboard.png)

## Business Questions Answered
The dashboard was designed to deliver quick responses to essential business inquiries:
* What are the total revenue, profit, and order volume generated?
* Which product categories and sub-categories are the top performers?
* How does revenue evolve over time, and is there visible seasonality?
* Which geographic regions and specific states drive the most profit and volume?
* Who are the top-performing customers?

## Key Business Insights (Based on Dashboard Data)
1. **Overall Performance:** From 2014 to 2017, the company achieved **$2.30M in Total Revenue** and earned a substantial profit margin, totaling **$286.40K in Total Profit** across more than 5,000 orders.
2. **Category Leaders:** **Technology** remains the top revenue generator at $0.84M, followed closely by Furniture at $0.74M. Within these, "Phones" and "Chairs" stand out as the most profitable sub-categories.
3. **Geographic Dominance:** The **West Region** ($725K) and **East Region** ($679K) are the primary sources of the company's sales. Notably, **California ($458K)** and **New York ($311K)** significantly outperform other states.
4. **Sales Trends:** There is a clear seasonal trend, with massive revenue spikes occurring consistently in the fourth quarter (Q4) of every year.

##  Tools & Technical Stack
* **Tool:** Power BI Desktop
* **Calculations:** DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for dynamic KPI metrics.
* **Data Visualization:** Line charts for forecasting over time, interactive maps showing geographic data (with bubble size indicating revenue), and a tailored dark-mode UI/UX for better readability and contrast.
* **Interactivity:** Integrated slicers for Year, Region, and Customer Segment allowing for granular drill-downs.
